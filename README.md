An Empathy Map captures what a user says, thinks, does, and feels about a particular experience or situation. It puts the team “in the user’s shoes” to design better solutions.

⸻

💡 Purpose
	•	To understand users’ motivations and pain points.
	•	To guide design and decision-making.
	•	To align team understanding around user needs.
	•	To build empathy within teams.
